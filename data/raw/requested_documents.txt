Experiencing issues?
If you are experiencing problems when uploading a file, please check if your file meets the following requirements:
The file can be maximum 15 MB (and minimum 4 MB).
Make sure pictures are saved under .JPG-format.
Make sure the pdf is not protected.
Make sure
there are no spaces in the filename
the filename is not too long
the filename only uses Roman characters
the filename doesn't contain diacritical characters
Protected documents may cause problems, because our system cannot process them. Some educational institutions or other organisations (like ETS) encrypt their documents, which prevents them from being altered. One way around this issue is to print your score report, scan the score report, and then upload the scan to our application portal. You can also unlock your documents using various tools available online.
You can also try clearing the cache and cookies.
Use a different web browser (we recommend Google Chrome).
