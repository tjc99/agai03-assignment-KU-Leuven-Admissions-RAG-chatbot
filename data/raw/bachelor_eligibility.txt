Applicants seeking admission to a
bachelor's programme
can verify whether their secondary qualification meets the minimum eligibility criteria. Please note that other elements may be requested in the admissions process, such as language or mathematics tests. To check these specific requirements, it is required to consult the admission requirements of the
bachelor's programme
of your choice. The minimum eligibility requirements will be updated before the start of each application period.
Meeting these criteria means you are eligible to apply for our courses, but it does not guarantee selection.
Admission can only be obtained by following the
application process
.
